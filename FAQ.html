FAQ

Which Martial Arts Can I Study?

Any martial arts that calls to you! Each form of martial arts offers different fighting techniques, but all martial arts offer the same philosophies.

I'm unfit. Can I still try it?

No one is born fit. We all work towards being fit and this is not different. Start small and gradually work your way up.

Am I too old for this?

Absolutely not. Some of the oldest students I've seen are in their mid 60s. If they can do it, so can you!

Will I get hurt?

Risk of injury is common in any type of sport. Martial arts emphasizes on control, which decreases most situations that can cause injuries to be drastically reduced.
